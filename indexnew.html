Cultural Evening is back for another year of showcasing the best, most diverse talents, cuisine and cultures that St Olave's has to offer. Come along for a guaranteed evening of music, dance, and more. And food.

What's going on? Why am I here?

Cultural Evening is St Olave's yearly event celebrating the cultural diversity that is the lifeblood of St Olaves, before exams start and everybody is assigned a candidate number. Continue reading for information about tickets, times and dates.

Where can I buy tickets?

A limited number of tickets will be available outside the Sixth Form Common Room door. They will also be sold online, via sQuid. Prices are {unconfirmed} for students and {unconfirmed} for adults. Note that this year, there will be no ticket sales on the door. Seriously. So make sure you buy your tickets ASAP to ensure yourself and your families/friends/guests/random strangers off the street, places in our Great Hall on the night.

Ticket sales close on the Thursday, 17th of November.

When is Cultural Evening?

Cultural Evening takes place on the Saturday, 19th of November. Guests are welcome from 6:30 PM onwards. Participants MUST arrive by 1:00 PM for a rehearsal and to help set up.

The event itself begins at 7:00 PM.
