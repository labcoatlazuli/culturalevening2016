Cultural Evening is back for another year of showcasing the best, most diverse talents, cuisine and cultures that St Olave's has to offer. Come along for a guaranteed evening of music, dance, fashion and food.

What's going on? Why am I here?

Cultural Evening is St Olave's yearly event celebrating the cultural diversity that is the lifeblood of St Olaves. Continue reading for information about tickets, times and dates.

Where can I buy tickets?

A limited number of tickets will be available outside the Sixth Form Common Room door. They will also be sold online, via sQuid. Prices are £8 for students and £10 for adults. Note that this year, there will be no ticket sales on the door. So make sure you buy your tickets ASAP to ensure yourself and your families/friends/guests places in our Great Hall on the night.

Ticket sales close on the Thursday, 17th of November.
